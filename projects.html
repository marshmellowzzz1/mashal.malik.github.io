Creative Currency Project

A project I researched on before creating a new type of currency. I used my Photoshop and Illustrator skills to design this bill for bookstores in Toronto. My research outlined the value and use of this currency within society and it's design process. I used my own pictures to design this bill to show creativity and originality.
